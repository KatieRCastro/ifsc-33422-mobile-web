About Guatemala

Guatemala, officially the Republic of Guatemala, is a country located in Central America. Bordered by Mexico to the north and west, Belize to the northeast, Honduras to the east, and El Salvador to the southeast, Guatemala is a land of incredible natural beauty, ancient ruins, and colorful traditions.
